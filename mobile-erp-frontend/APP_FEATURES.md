# Application Features Documentation

## 1. Inventory Management
*   **IMEI Tracking**: Real-time tracking of mobile devices using unique IMEI numbers.
*   **Multi-Branch Support**: Monitor stock levels across different retail branches.
*   **GSM Archive**: Sync models and specifications from our global database.
*   **Incident Reporting**: Report damaged or stolen devices directly from the inventory list.

## 2. Sales & POS
*   **Interactive POS**: Fast-paced point-of-sale terminal with keyboard shortcuts (F2/F8/F9/F10).
*   **Customer Integration**: Support for walk-in and registered customers with history tracking.
*   **Invoice Management**: Edit existing sales and view detailed transaction histories.

## 3. Procurement
*   **Purchase Tracking**: Record vendor arrivals and manage supplier balances.
*   **Auto-Inventory Update**: Automatically update stock levels and IMEI status upon purchase.

## 4. Financial Accounting
*   **General Ledger**: Audit trail of all financial transactions with date filtering.
*   **Cash/Bank Management**: Track liquid reserves across different accounts.

## 5. Reseller Portal
*   **Territory Management**: Resellers can manage their own clients and view performance metrics.
*   **Promo Codes**: Assign and track promos for various territories.

## 6. Business Intelligence
*   **Sales Trends**: Visual representation of revenue over time using interactive charts.
*   **Stock Audits**: Detailed breakdown of assets and their valuations.
*   **HR Commissions**: Ledger to manage staff performance and commission payouts.
